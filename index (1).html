The Consultation Experience

A clear path, taken one step at a time

01

Information gathering

The initial consultation is an in-depth exploration of you as a whole — medical and family history, lifestyle and environmental influences, emotional wellbeing, and personal health objectives, beginning with a pre-screening form. Where clinically appropriate, functional testing may be recommended to inform a comprehensive understanding.

02

A personalised health strategy

Following the consultation, Neelkamal develops a bespoke plan aligned with your physiology, lifestyle, and long-term goals — targeted nutritional guidance, premium supplementation, lifestyle refinement, mind-body techniques, and preventative strategies, each selected with intention and clinical insight.

03

Ongoing care and maintenance

Follow-up consultations provide continuity, refinement, and support as your health evolves. As balance is restored, the focus shifts to longevity, resilience, and the cultivation of sustainable habits that support enduring wellbeing.

About the Practitioner

Neelkamal

With a lifelong passion for medical science, Neelkamal offers a discreet, science-led approach to personalised healthcare. Her academic foundation includes a Pharmacy degree and postgraduate studies in Clinical Research Management at Cranfield University in the UK, where she honed expertise in evidence-based medicine, clinical analysis, and drug safety evaluation.

Through extensive professional experience in the UK healthcare system and clinical practice in Mauritius, Neelkamal recognised that many chronic conditions could be improved — or even reversed — through preventative, natural approaches. This insight inspired her to expand into naturopathic nutrition, blending conventional medical science with holistic principles.

Each consultation is carefully tailored, moving beyond symptom management to uncover root causes and foster resilience — a personalised, high-touch experience designed to support a healthier, more harmonious life.
